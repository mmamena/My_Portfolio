Gluten Free General Tso’s Chicken

INGREDIENTS

  1. 3 tbsp canola oil (divided)
  2. ¼ cup Gluten-Free Soy Sauce
  3. 2 tbsp rice vinegar
  4. 1 tbsp sriracha
  5. 2 tbsp honey
  6. 1 tbsp fresh garlic (crushed)
  7. 2 tsp fresh ginger (grated)
  8. ½ tsp chinese five spice
  9. 1 tsp crushed red pepper flakes
  10. 1 lb boneless skinless chicken breasts
    (sliced into large chunks)
  11. ½ cup cornstarch
  12. ½ tsp salt
  13. ½ tsp black pepper
  14. 8 small dried red chilies
  15. ¼ cup scallions (sliced)
  • In a medium bowl, whisk 1 tbsp canola oil with the soy sauce,rice vinegar, sriracha, honey, garlic, ginger,
    chinese five spice and red pepper flakes. Set aside.
  • Toss the chicken breasts in the cornstarch, salt and pepper. Add the remaining 2 tbsp canola oil to a large skillet over medium-high heat.
  • When the oil is hot, add the chicken and cook 4-5 minutes. Add the chilies, then pour the sauce over the chicken and cook 2-3 minutes.
  • Add the scallions, cook an additional minute,remove from the stove and serve over rice.
